About Me

I’m Erik, founder and owner of Eureka Tutoring! I am currently a 2nd year student at McMaster University studying Software Engineering & Neuroscience. Ever since I was a little kid I’ve had a passion for learning and an even bigger love for sharing what I learn with others. I was extremely privileged to have teachers throughout my childhood who excited and challenged me through their teaching, and my goal is to foster that same love for learning in the next generation of students. Throughout my time teaching both math and swimming I have worked with hundreds of students of various ages and backgrounds, and I understand that every kid is different, each with their own preferred learning styles and needs. I personally assure that I will do everything I can to not only improve your child's academic performance but also help cultivate a passion for learning that will carry them through their academic journey and beyond!
